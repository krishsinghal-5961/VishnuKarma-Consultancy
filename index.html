What We Offer

End-to-End Project Management Solutions

Our services provide complete coverage of the project management lifecycle — from pre-construction planning through final account settlement.

01
Pre Tender & Post Tender Works
End-to-end advisory from bid preparation through contract award and post-tender execution.
  • Technical evaluation of bids and contractor submissions
  • Project planning, programme development & baseline scheduling
  • Budget preparation and pre-tender cost analysis
  • Cost control measures and post-award budget management
  • Tender documentation review and BOQ analysis
02
Project Monitoring & Control
Systematic tracking of site execution against planned targets, with corrective action protocols.
  • Site progress monitoring and execution tracking
  • Earned Value Management (EVM) analysis
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly MIS reporting
  • Stakeholder progress presentations
  • Interface and coordination management
03
Contract Administration
Comprehensive support for all contractual obligations, from bill certification to dispute avoidance.
  • Contract review and interpretation
  • BOQ review and quantity management
  • Contractor bill verification and certification
  • Variation order management and valuation
  • Contractual letter drafting and correspondence
04
Claims & EOT Advisory
Specialist analysis and preparation of extension of time and prolongation cost claims.
  • Delay analysis — Impacted As-Planned, TIA
  • EOT claim preparation and response
  • Prolongation and disruption cost assessment
  • Claims defence and mitigation strategies
  • Dispute resolution support
05
Cost & Commercial Control
Financial management from pre-tender budget through final project account reconciliation.
  • Pre-tender cost analysis and budgeting
  • Post-award budget and cost control
  • Cash flow management and forecasting
  • Cost reporting and financial MIS
  • Final account preparation and negotiation
06
Risk & Quality Management
Structured frameworks for identifying, mitigating, and monitoring project risks and quality standards.
  • Risk register development and tracking
  • Delay and interface risk mitigation planning
  • QA/QC framework implementation
  • Statutory and regulatory compliance monitoring
  • Safety and environmental compliance oversight
